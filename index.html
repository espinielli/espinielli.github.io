DVD to Android Tablet

2016 April 2 00:00 UTC DVD, android
How to transfer your DVDs to your Android Tablet Suppose your kids want to watch their DVDs in the car on their Android tablet… Here is what you can do (on a Mac OSX 10.9.5): install Handbrake and its CLI $ brew cask install handbrake $ brew cask install handbrakecli install libdvdcss (thanks to lifehacker’s article) in order to bypass copy protection $ brew install libdvdcss go to the place where you will place the ripped DVD: $ cd ~/Movies convert your DVDs: $ HandBrakeCLI -i /Volumes/'my DVD' -o /Volumes/Untitled/Movies/my_dvd.
